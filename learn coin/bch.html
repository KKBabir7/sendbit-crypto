What is Bitcoin Cash?

Bitcoin Cash is a fork of Bitcoin that aims to add more transaction capacity to the network to be useful in daily transactions.

When was it created?

Bitcoin Cash was created by a community of developers looking to increase Bitcoin's block size on August 1, 2017.

What can you do with it?

You can hold the Litecoin currency you own as an investment, sell it, send it to someone, or convert it directly to Bitcoin.
